Barangay Banay-Banay

Barangay Banay-Banay is one of the barangays of City of Cabuyao which is currently led by Cabuyao City MAYOR Dennis Felipe 'DenHa' Hain. The Barangay itself is led by Barangay Captain Eric Barron. Banay-Banay's population is said to be 34,260 as determined by 2020 Census and represents 9.64% of the total population of Cabuyao.

Region: CALABARZON (Region IV-A)

Province: Laguna

Postal code: 4025

Sources: PhilAtlas, Banay-Banay Facebook Page, Wikipedia
